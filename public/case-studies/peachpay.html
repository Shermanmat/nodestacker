CASE STUDY · 2023 · FINTECH

PeachPay closed a $379K seed round through a single MatCap intro.

All-in-one payments and checkout solution.

FOUNDER
Aaryaman Anerao
SECTOR
Fintech · Payments
STAGE
Seed
LEAD INVESTOR
Jeremy Kuo
RAISED
$379K
MATCAP'S ROLE
Inbound discovery + manual intro

The challenge

Aaryaman didn't want to pay subscription fees or finder's fees to find investors — but he needed quality intros to close his round. He needed a path where the people on the other end took the meeting on the merits, not because someone was paid to send it.

What MatCap did

  • Surfaced PeachPay to investors browsing the platform.
  • Made manual intros to Jeremy Kuo and Chris McCann (Race Capital) when they showed interest.
  • Stayed out of the way — fast intros, no friction.

The outcome

  • $379K from Jeremy Kuo.
  • Seed round closed shortly after.
  • Founder kept full control of process and equity.
