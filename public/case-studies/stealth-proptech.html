The challenge

The founder was reintroduced to MatCap through another portfolio founder. Mat had known him years before from his coding days, but the reconnection was as a fresh, first-time startup founder — nervous about venture, with deep industry knowledge but no investor network and no fundraising playbook.

He needed both education and conviction-backed intros — and a pace that would let him close a real round without burning out before he started.

What MatCap did

  • Invited the founder to an AZVC event for first VC exposure.
  • Hosted him on the AZTF Live livestream — public credibility moment.
  • Began advising formally.
  • First intro: Elizabeth Yin (Hustle Fund) → $150K check.
  • Built momentum with intros to Josh Constine (angel) and a local family office.
  • Anchored the round locally with Mike Randall (Monsoon Venture Fund).
  • Two additional angel checks via direct Mat-to-angel intros (held until announcement).
  • Post-round: helped plan capital deployment for long-term durability — not the founder's natural instinct after a fresh raise.

The outcome

  • $600K pre-seed round closed.
  • 5 checks wired through MatCap intros — institutional (Hustle Fund, Monsoon, Copper Sky · Jack Selby) plus three prominent angels.
  • Tier 1 firms (Benchmark, Founders Fund, Matrix, NFX, First Round) took the meeting. None of them wrote a check. Hustle Fund did.
  • Relationship continues through future rounds — seed, A, B and beyond.
