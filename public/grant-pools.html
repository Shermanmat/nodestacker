How it works

Mat puts in first. Then he brings others.

A grant pool is simple: an effort worth supporting, a personal commitment from Mat to get it moving, and an open door for others to add to the pool. No equity, no terms — just collective backing for something that should exist.

We find an effort

A project, tool, community, or piece of public good worth bringing to life — startup or not.

Mat commits first

Mat Sherman personally puts money or resources in — skin in the game before anyone else.

Others pool in

We open it up — anyone who believes in the effort can add to the pool and help it cross the line.
