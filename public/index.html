Know how to run the room

Getting the meeting is half the battle. Running it is the other half.

So we train you for both.

🎭

Practice against realistic AI VCs

Step into the Pitch Gym and pitch a range of lifelike investor personas — a fund GP, a junior associate at a small fund, a check-writer at a big hedge fund, an angel. Each reacts differently, just like the real thing. Reps any time, with a blunt readout on what landed and what didn't.

We get you set up on Granola so every investor call is captured. After each one, we review the transcript and send you specific coaching — exactly what to tighten before the next.

The better you get in the room, the more investors say yes — and the more your pace climbs.
