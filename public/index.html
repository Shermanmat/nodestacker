How we work

Your fundraise point guard.

You're the front man — you pitch, you own the raise. We're behind the scenes making it easier. We don't run your raise. We make you better at it.

Warm intros to the right local angels and coastal VCs — the ones we've already built trust with. You walk in already vouched for.
