The intro engine

Access is the hard part. Here's how we get you it.

Most founders don't lose because the company is weak. They lose because they can't get in the room. The intro engine is the same playbook, run for every founder we work with.

  1. 01
    Draft the blurb.

    We sharpen your one-paragraph pitch until it's tight and forwardable — the thing an investor reads in ten seconds and says "send them over."

  2. 02
    Send it to the right investor. If they accept, we make the intro.

    We send your blurb to the investors who actually fit your stage, sector, and check size. Once they opt in, we make the intro for you two to get to know each other — warm and vouched for, never a cold email.

  3. 03
    Coach through the raise.

    Online sessions with your peers and one-on-one office hours with us. Deck, signaling, term sheets, when to push and when to walk — you don't learn the patterns on your own round.
