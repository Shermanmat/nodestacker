METHODOLOGY

How we count.

  • Intros made — every intro request submitted through the MatCap platform, all-time. Includes the prior Seedscout era.
  • Accept rate — share of intros where the investor opted in (introduced, first meeting, follow-up, circle-back, or invested), divided by all responded intros (excludes pending).
  • Unique firms — distinct investor firms that took at least one intro in the trailing 6 months.
  • Investors in network — investor records currently in the MatCap database. Active and inactive combined.
  • Founders worked with — distinct founders who have used the MatCap intro process.
  • Privacy — no individual founder, company, or investor performance is published. All numbers are aggregate.
