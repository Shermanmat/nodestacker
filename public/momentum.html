How MatCap Works

Momentum.

MatCap sends warm intro requests to investors on your behalf. You don't chase investors — they come to you. We build your fundraising momentum and adjust it to what's working: easing off when the story needs sharpening, pushing hard when investors lean in.

The play

This is the pre-seed playbook. Pre-seed is about finding your one believer — the first check that anchors the round. (Seed is a different game entirely: running a tight auction and stacking momentum to drive terms.) Everything below is built to get you that first believer.

The model

Five ways we build your momentum.

  1. 01
    Get moving
    Start with intro requests.

    We send warm intro requests to investors on your behalf — the outreach we control, run for you. You stop chasing investors. They start coming to you.

  2. 02
    Find the pace
    Set your pace.

    Every founder is different, so we start by learning yours. An initial burst of intro requests tells us what resonates — then we dial in a pace that fits you, not a template.

  3. 03
    Speed up
    Accelerate when it's working.

    When investors are leaning in and responding, we build on it — more intro requests, more conversations, more momentum. We push while you have it.

  4. 04
    Ease off
    Coaching when it's not.

    If investors aren't biting, that's a messaging signal — not a failure. We ease off and get you reps in the Pitch Gym: practice your pitch and time to sharpen the story, then ramp back up. A slow week is a redirect, never a penalty.

  5. 05
    Full speed
    Blitz when you land a lead.

    The moment a real investor commits, we throw fuel on the fire — a burst of intro requests to help you close the round while you have heat. Momentum compounds momentum.
