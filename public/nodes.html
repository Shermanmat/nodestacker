What is a node?

A node is anyone with meaningful investor relationships who wants to use them to help founders raise capital. You see our full founder portfolio and facilitate warm introductions to the investors you know personally.

Operators & Executives

CTOs, VPs, and founders who built companies and maintain strong relationships with the investors who funded them.

Angels & Advisors

Active angel investors and advisors embedded in the venture ecosystem who sit across multiple cap tables.

Associates & Students

Hyper-networked students at top programs and associates at firms who know the right people and want to build a track record as a connector.

How it works

01

See our full portfolio

As a node, you get access to every founder in the MatCap portfolio. Browse companies, stages, and sectors — and decide which founders you can help.

02

Make warm introductions

When you see a founder who fits an investor you know personally, you facilitate the warm introduction. Just bcc us so we can track all intro communications.

03

Build your track record

Every successful introduction builds your reputation at MatCap and in venture broadly. Compensation may be considered once enterprise value is created.
