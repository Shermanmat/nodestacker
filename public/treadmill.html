How MatCap Works

The Treadmill.

MatCap sends warm intro requests to investors on your behalf. You don't chase investors — they come to you. Think of it as a treadmill: the belt is the pace of intro requests we send, and it responds to what's working.

The model

Five gears of momentum.

  1. 01
    Step on
    Start with intro requests.

    We send warm intro requests to investors on your behalf — the outreach we control, run for you. You stop chasing investors. They start coming to you.

  2. 02
    Find the pace
    Set your pace.

    Every founder is different, so we start by learning yours. An initial burst of intro requests tells us what resonates — then we dial in a pace that fits you, not a template.

  3. 03
    Speed up
    Accelerate when it's working.

    When investors are leaning in and responding, the belt speeds up — more intro requests, more conversations, more momentum. We push while you have it.

  4. 04
    Ease off
    Coaching when it's not.

    If investors aren't biting, that's a messaging signal — not a failure. We ease off the gas and get you reps: pitch practice and time to sharpen the story, then back on. A slow week is a redirect, never a penalty.

  5. 05
    Full speed
    Blitz when you land a lead.

    The moment a real investor commits, we throw fuel on the fire — a burst of intro requests to help you close the round while you have heat. Momentum compounds momentum.
