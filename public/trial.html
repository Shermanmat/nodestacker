Let's see if we're a fit

This starts with your blurb. We let in founders we think investors in our network would enjoy meeting. Put your best foot forward here, show off a little, and we'll do our best to dream with you. Whether or not you get accepted, we hope this is a useful exercise for you.

1

Build your blurb

Use our blurb builder to put together how you'd want an investor to hear about you. It takes a few minutes, and you walk away with something useful regardless of what happens next.

2

Our team reviews and reaches out

We personally review your blurb and send intro requests to investors in our network who are a match. These are intro requests coming from people the investors already trust — not cold emails.

3

We both decide

No paperwork, no obligations. You see real investor responses and we see how the network reacts. Either side can walk away at any point — this is about figuring out if there's a real fit.

4

If there's momentum, we make it official

If intros are landing and we're both feeling it, we formalize with 1% equity. You're not obligated — but don't start the trial unless you'd be open to that if real value is being created.
