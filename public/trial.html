How MatCap works

A simple trial so you can see real results before committing to anything.

1

Build your investor blurb

Use our AI blurb builder to craft a pitch based on your strongest signals. It takes a few minutes and you'll walk away with a polished blurb whether or not you continue with MatCap.

2

Mat sends it to investors

Mat Sherman, founder of MatCap, personally reviews your blurb and sends it to relevant investors in our network. These are warm intros through people who already know and trust the investors.

3

See real results

This is a trial. No paperwork, no obligations. You see real investor responses and decide if MatCap is right for you. Either side can walk away at any point.

4

If it's working, we formalize

If intros are landing and there's momentum, we'd offer a long-term relationship in exchange for 1% equity in your company. You're not obligated to accept — but please don't start the trial unless you'd be open to that arrangement assuming value is being created.
