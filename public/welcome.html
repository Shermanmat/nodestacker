We don't charge fees. We don't take board seats. We join your cap table.

Companies become members by granting MatCap 1% equity. In return, we open our network to help you build.

Our success is your success.

02

The Node Network

MatCap is built around a group we call Nodes—experienced founders, operators, and investors who open doors for companies in the network.

When you join, you're introduced to Nodes who may resonate with what you're building. If they believe in your company, they make introductions, offer guidance, and expand your reach.

Nodes who create meaningful value may receive a share of our equity stake. Incentives aligned.
